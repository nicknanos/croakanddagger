About the Game

Croak and Dagger is a story-driven, scrolling platformer game blending exploration, and heartfelt narrative. When an evil witch curses a young couple in the heart of an ancient forest, their lives are forever changed. The hero, transformed into a lizard-like fighter, must embark on a perilous journey to save their partner, who has been turned into a frog. Navigate through enchanting yet dangerous landscapes and face challenging foes to confront the witch and undo her curse. Featuring stunning pixel art inspired by classic games, an emotional story, and engaging gameplay, Croak and Dagger invites you to experience a tale of courage, sacrifice, and the enduring power of love. Will you embrace your new form?
